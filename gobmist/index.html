Goblins in the Mists

You had heard rumors of ancient adventure archives hidden in the orcish caves of Dracon Warren, from the patrons at the Blue Frog Tavern, perhaps the orchiving Taran himself muttered about the labor of love he carefully collected and stashed in his dwelling. The room whose junk piles you are exploring rewards you with one such tome: the edited account of one such dungeon delve undertaken many years ago. Behold, it is one of several books in existence, and it contains within its pages the infamous quest to rid the city of The Goblins in the Mists!

Goblins in the Mists was a Tunnels & Trolls play-by-post adventure written at The Blue Frog Tavern, a (now defunct) Yahoo! Club, during the months of February through July in the fake millenial year 2000. I have archived and organized the posts into chapters for your reading at this site.
