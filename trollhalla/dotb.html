Orchivist's Note: Argh, another adventure for the Orchive! This archive employs such high tech functionality as separate pages for each scene. Whoa!

So far the artwork is stolen directly from the Trollhalla Hall of Champions. I saved the avatars each player was using at the time the adventure started. Anyone wishing to draw a scene of our brave adventurers may submit it to me and I will add it to the archive.

The game master for turns 1 through 14, and 16 through 27 was Khenn Arrth (Ken St. Andre).
The game master for turn 15 was Corencio (James Corwin St. Andre).
The game master for turns 28 through 35 was Gimor Ironfang (Kurt Romig).

It seems from the game text of scene 35 that Gimor tried to hand the game back over to Khenn, but I don't think this hand-off was successful as there were no moves posted by any GM after scene 35.

Finally, as I did not record the attributes for each character at the onset of the adventure, the character cards start out pretty bare, but as attributes are revealed in-game, I add them to the cards.

Enjoy the adventure!

Stinkily,
Taran Dracon the ORC! [thus signed and updated July 16, 2010]
