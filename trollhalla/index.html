What is a Trollhalla adventure, you ask? It is a Tunnels & Trolls adventure run in a Play-By-E-mail format by Ken St. Andre, using the characters of members of Trollhalla, Ken's T&T enthusiasts club. As part of the "Trollhalla Elite," I was privy to, and sometimes played in, these adventures, and knew they were too good to not be shared with the world. This is the mission of this page.

Read ye herewith the following Trollhalla adventures!

  1. November 2005: Goblin Quest, starring Boozer the Goblin
  2. December 2005 - March 2006: The Adventures of Dandelion the Ogre in the Moonstone Caves
  3. March 2006 - July 2006: Minions of Trollhalla vs. The Dungeon of the Bear (complete archive of unfinished adventure)
  4. July 2006 - September 2006: Dehk's Saga (complete archive of unfinished adventure)
  5. January 2007: The Adventures of Boozer in Khazan
