Dekh's Saga

Dekh wasn't cold anymore. In fact, he wasn't sure what he was - other than buck-naked that is. He stood on a stony, bare-dirt plain which was littered with decaying corpses. He managed to scavenge a buckler and a scimitar that weren't too useless. Above him, the black sky held no stars, just a pale moonlike object which gave barely enough light to see. The silence was rent by a booming voice that seemed to come from everywhere and nowhere at the same time.

"According to the First Agreement with the Nameless One, thou may regain thy life and soul by braving the Abyss. Thy way is dark. May thy fate be better."

Dekh became aware that he was standing on a rough cobblestone path.
