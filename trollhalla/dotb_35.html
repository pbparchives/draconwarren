Turn 1 - Turn 34

"AAARRRTTHH!!" The TrollGod’s name reverberated down the corridor as the two mighty trolls charged at the urooks. They were either too brave (or too stupid) to realize that they should have run away. Instead, the two lead warriors tried to protect the archer.

Quogh’s mighty smash with the ram-headed maul killed one urook outright. His momentum carried him into the archer, and the crossbow was knocked out of his grasp. Vin’s inexperience cost him dearly. The urook was quickly inside his reach, and a long nasty cut across his chest bit deeply.

[Quogh--439 (4 spite) / urook - 263 (0 spite) / archer missed L4SR vs STR70 / Vin--223 (6 spite) / urook--301 (16 spite)]

Vin realized he was in trouble--this urook was tougher than he’d hoped. But it had made a mistake getting in close. Vin made a desperate grab and managed to grab the urook. The scimitar cut him as he wrapped his arms around his enemy, but that would be the last thing it would be doing. Vin could feel the bones breaking as he sqeezed. Quog found himself nearly on top of the last urook as it valiantly drew its scimitar. It didn’t matter against the mighty southern troll--two blows, two dead urooks.

[Quogh--435 (2 spite) / urook - 248 (4 spite) / Vin makes L15SRvs DEX, so--195 (0 spite) / urook--181 (1 spite, plus 12 "spite" from the scimitar)]

"Quogh! This one’s not quite dead yet. Do we want him alive?"
