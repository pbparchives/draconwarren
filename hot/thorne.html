Background/Personality:

Thorne is somewhat slight, tall and fair, but hides his light: he dresses in dark, almost sooty brown; prefers a hooded cloak but cinches the cloak at the waist. He wears no ornament. (He probably has none.) He's citified, as elves go. It's a little hard to picture him cavorting in any glades. He's friendly enough in a quiet way; he's given to a thin smile. He calls the stinks and sweats of Khazan home more than the forests of his kindred, and speaks little of his past. He's cut himself from it, and is bent on carving a way for himself. He's as comfortable with orc as he is with elf. Lately, frankly, he's been thieving, and is trying to decide whether the call for the hands of thieves is an opportunity to go straight, or to cut down on competition. He'll wait and see.
